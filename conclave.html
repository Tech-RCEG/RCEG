ABOUT CONCLAVE

True to its name , conclave organised by RCEG is a kind of a formal conference or summit wherein the pioneers who reached great heights in their lives share their experiences to guide the posterity. The professional speeches and the incentive orations made this event to resemble the popular Tedx talk show, infact the students in College of Engineering Guindy consider this event as a mini tedx talk show. Thanks to the constant support of students and the progressive activities of the club members , for the sake of which the event was esteemed as the inspiring ongoing project award in 2015.
