ABOUT RCEG

Carrying the legacy of being the Oldest club, the Rotaract club of College of Engineering Guindy well known as RCEG strives to bring together the students of CEG through its various activities and services pertaining to the community and the society with the support of the parent club ‘Rotary Club of Madras South’. The RCEG focusses on four avenues of services that include Club service, Professional service, International service and Community service.  It has never failed to showcase its latent potential and marks its place through the signature events like ICE, Vizhithiru, Conclave and Sangarsh each and every year in its calendar. This year, RCEG is gearing up with the theme “IMAGINE BELIEVE ACHIEVE” - our thoughts, motivation and actions pay the way for success.

Club Service

Through this service RCEG intends to provide platform for indigenous talents of CEG. It serves as a gateway for the CEGians to get their place and bond with RCEGians to strive for the society. It often conducts the Intra College Events, shortly called ICE like online photography, boomerang, etc. The significant event called Pattanathil Pattikaadu, a celebration of  Pongal comprises of various traditional events like tug of war, sugarcane eating, sack race and others of such kind. All these are primarily done to keep the club and CEGians animated.

Professional Service

This avenue of service paves way for the RCEGians to work towards the inculcation of professional skills that are inevitable in this century of life. The two important events of this service are Vizhithiru, the one in which the members of the club extend their service to the school students to make their dreams come true and Conclave, a talkshow meant for converging the minds of college community. Through these events, the RCEGians are bestowed with the organisational skills steadily.

Community Service

All the great leaders of the world are noted for their yeoman services. Helping hand is the greatest weapon used in the society that is left helpless. Such an attitude is cultivated in the minds of RCEGians through this service. Sangarsh stands as a testimony each and every year fighting for a cause through a concert i.e.) a concert for a cause. Fund is raised through a concert to support the noble cause which makes RCEG to outstand all other clubs.

International Service

Good things spread slowly but firmly. Similarly, RCEG extends its service universally by International service.The main aim of International Service in Rotary is expressed in the fourth object of Rotary, namely, to encourage and foster the advancement of international understanding, goodwill, and peace through a world fellowship of business and professional persons united in the ideal of service.

Recent Events

Vizhithiru

Vizhithiru has been investing on enthusiastic children from government schools and helping them to chisel their intelligence and equip themselves towards a streamlined destination. Vizhithiru with the theme of Kanavu Meipada intends to replace an empty mind with an open one.

Alumni Meet

Listen to your elders not because they are always right but because they might have experiences of being wrong. And the best way to move in right path does not always lie in choosing the right direction but in having the knowledge about the wrong path. On this note RCEG always feels blessed to return to its roots . Yes, every year RCEG blissfully invites its alumni by an event tagged as "Alumni meet "to learn from our forerunners.

Athiban

In today’s competitive world, many people race for seeking opportunities but among them, few are gems known as Enterpreneurs who create those opportunities for themselves and for the benefit of others. In this regard, the Rotaract club of CEG feels proud to collaborate with our parent club The Rotary club of Madras South and to be a part of the great initiative AATHIBAN in RYLA which aims at fostering entrepreneurship skills in budding entrepreneurs

Pattanathil Pattikadu

All work and no play makes John a dull boy . Indeed, it is equally essential to have some fun inspite of all the work we do. Hence it requires a break to relax, refresh ourselves for better performance ahead. What if these extravaganzas depict our cultural traditions, doesn't it sound great? Yes, it is! Thats why we Rotaract club of CEG presents the most spectacular, joyous event Pattanathil Pattikaadu.
